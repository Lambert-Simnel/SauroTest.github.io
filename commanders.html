Scout

Sauromatia can get these in any province they own.
Not because they are foreign-rec, but because you should have a fort on every province you own.

Chieftain

These are good only because they are foreign-rec MM3 commanders.
In a tight spot you can get some to move around foreign-rec cavalry units.

Sauromatian Manflayer

Makes a decent commander.
You’ll most likely go without ever recruiting one.
This is because they require fort turns, and chieftains do not.

Warrior Priestess

As above makes a decent priest commander.
You might want to recruit a few to build temples and bless Oiorpata.
Later on you’ll want to to recruit indie priests for those jobs and for blood sacrificing.
But for leading troops, Sauromatian Manflayers are simply better.

Warrior Queen

Makes a good leader.
You don’t want any of these.

Hydra Tamer

Don’t.
